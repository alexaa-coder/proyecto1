ORDER AND CUSTOMER MANAGEMENT Aim
The purpose of this procedure is to define the management of customers and orders to which Spika Tech, hereinafter Spika, distributes its products, encompassing the process of registering, modifying, and deleting said customers and orders.
Scope This procedure applies to all SPIKA clients.
Responsibilities and Departments Affected It is the Sales department's responsibility to request and maintain up-to-date customer and order documentation.
It is the responsibility of the Quality department to safeguard the documentation generated by the registration, cancellation, or modification of clients.
It is the responsibility of the Sales department to safeguard the documentation generated from orders.

Client Updates and Maintenance Sales staff will be able to receive customer feedback and update any required registration documents. This process is vital because up-to-date information is essential in the event of a recall.
System feedback In relation to system feedback in order to improve the quality of products and the service provided, SPIKA makes available to customers through the web portal the possibility of transmitting complaints, see more details in PNT-007 Complaints Management and it is SPIKA's job to measure customer satisfaction, through PNT-009 Customer Satisfaction Measure .
