MEASURE OF CUSTOMER SATISFACTION Target
The aim of this procedure is to describe the system to be used to carry out the Customer Satisfaction Measurement for the medical devices produced by Spika Tech, hereinafter SPIKA.
Outreach This procedure applies to all medical devices marketed by SPIKA.
Responsibilities and Departments concerned Management and Sales should follow up on the measure of customer satisfaction.
The departments concerned are listed in the table below:
Definitions and Acronyms IRSGC: Quality Management System Review Report.
Procedure Introduction
On an annual basis, questionnaires will be sent out by the Sales Department in order to measure the satisfaction of the customers to whom SPIKA supplies products.
A selection of the customers with the highest turnover will be made and the questionnaire included in Annex 1 will be sent to them and returned to the Responsible Technician. The selection of customers and follow-up of the questionnaires will be carried out by the Sales area.
In this annual evaluation SPIKA will assess the post-production activities by reviewing the following records:
List of customer complaints, to be documented in the IRSGCD Customer satisfaction questionnaires
Sending questionnaires to customers.
With the list of customers with the highest turnover, the Sales staff will send the "Customer Satisfaction Questionnaire" (see Annex 1) by post or e-mail to the customers indicated in the list, in order to be able to evaluate the quality of the product and service.
The "Customer Satisfaction Questionnaire" includes 3 sections for the assessment of:
Product Satisfaction.
Service Satisfaction.
Overall satisfaction.
Assessment of the questionnaire Once the duly completed questionnaire has been received, it will be sent to the Responsible Technician. The questionnaires must be signed and dated, or, failing this, the communication of the sending of the questionnaire to SPIKA must be included.
With these questionnaires and the annual complaint lists, all information will be reviewed and conclusions will be collected in the IRSGC.
Communication to management The result of the customer satisfaction measurement shall be reported to the management in accordance with SOP-010 Management review of the effectiveness of the quality management system.
Distribution of the Procedure Staff belonging to the departments listed in Section 3 (Responsibilities and Departments concerned) should be provided with a copy of this procedure. In addition, controlled copy no. 1 will be given to the Archives for archiving on paper.
