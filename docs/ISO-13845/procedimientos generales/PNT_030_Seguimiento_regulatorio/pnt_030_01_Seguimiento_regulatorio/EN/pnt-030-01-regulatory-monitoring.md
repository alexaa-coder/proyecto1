REGULATORY MONITORING Aim
Ensure that SPIKA systematically evaluates and implements changes in standards, laws, and regulatory requirements applicable to its products, ensuring continued compliance with:
Regulation (EU) 2017/745 (MDR).
ISO 13485:2016.
Other applicable regulations (FDA, national, etc.).
Scope It applies to all of the organization's procedures related to the implemented quality management system.
Responsibilities and Departments Affected The affected departments are:
Definitions and Acronyms Regulatory change : Modification in applicable laws, regulations, standards or guidelines .
Impact assessment : Process to determine whether a regulatory change affects the organization's product, documentation or processes AEMPS : Spanish Agency for Medicines and Health Products .
BOE : Official State Gazette OJEU : Official Journal of the European Union
MDR: Medical Device Regulation NB : Notified Body
Procedure Identification of regulatory changes
To identify regulatory changes, active monitoring is carried out through periodic review of official sources:
Official Journal of the EU (quarterly)
AEMPS website (quarterly)
Publications of notified bodies (quarterly)
ISO Standards Updates (Quarterly)
Assessment of relevance and impact When a potential change is identified, the following actions are taken:
The detected change is recorded in the online regulatory change detection form.
The team responsible for the quality management system and regulatory documents performs a preliminary analysis to determine:
If it affects products (by type, class or market)
The degree of impact (high, medium or low)
The deadlines for implementing the change Planning and implementation
For changes that require action, a specific action plan is made that includes:
Documents to be updated (ETM, procedures, etc.).
Necessary process modifications.
Staff training needs.
Realistic implementation deadlines.
Verification and closure Once all the changes have been implemented to comply with the regulatory changes, the effectiveness of the change is verified by:
Internal audits Review of document samples
Surveys of trained personnel Distribution of the Procedure
