## **1. Aim**
Ensure that SPIKA systematically evaluates and implements changes in standards, laws, and regulatory requirements applicable to its products, ensuring continued compliance with:
- Regulation (EU) 2017/745 (MDR).
- ISO 13485:2016.
- Other applicable regulations (FDA, national, etc.).
## **2. Scope**
It applies to all of the organization's procedures related to the implemented quality management system.

## **3. Definitions and Acronyms**
- Regulatory change : Modification in applicable laws, regulations, standards or
guidelines .
- Impact assessment : Process to determine whether a regulatory change affects the
organization's product, documentation or processes
- AEMPS : Spanish Agency for Medicines and Health Products .
- BOE : Official State Gazette
- OJEU : Official Journal of the European Union
- MDR: Medical Device Regulation
- NB : Notified _Body_
## **4. Procedure** **4.1. Identification of regulatory changes**
To identify regulatory changes, active monitoring is carried out through periodic review of official sources:
- Official Journal of the EU (quarterly)
- AEMPS website (quarterly)
- Publications of notified bodies (quarterly)
- ISO Standards Updates (Quarterly)
## **4.2. Assessment of relevance and impact**
When a potential change is identified, the following actions are taken:
1. The detected change is recorded in the online regulatory change detection form.
2. The team responsible for the quality management system and regulatory documents performs a preliminary analysis to determine:
a. If it affects products (by type, class or market)

## **4.3. Planning and implementation**
For changes that require action, a specific action plan is made that includes:
- Documents to be updated (ETM, procedures, etc.).
- Necessary process modifications.
- Staff training needs.
- Realistic implementation deadlines.
## **4.4. Verification and closure**
Once all the changes have been implemented to comply with the regulatory changes, the effectiveness of the change is verified by:
- Internal audits
- Review of document samples
- Surveys of trained personnel
