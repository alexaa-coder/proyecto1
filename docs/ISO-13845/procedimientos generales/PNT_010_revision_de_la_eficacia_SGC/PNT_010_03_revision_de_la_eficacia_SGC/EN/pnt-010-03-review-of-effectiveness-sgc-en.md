MANAGEMENT REVIEW OF THE QUALITY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM Target
The aim of this procedure is to define the system to be followed for the review of the quality system by the Management of Spika Tech, hereinafter SPIKA, in order to assess the degree of compliance with the quality policy and objectives set through the established quality management system, in accordance with the applicable rules and regulations, as well as to set quality objectives for the following periods.
Outreach This procedure applies to the review of the quality system by SPIKA management.
Responsibilities and Departments concerned It is the responsibility of the Quality Manager to meet quarterly with management to review the objectives and effectiveness of the QMS.
It is the responsibility of management to meet with the Quality Manager annually to set annual QMS objectives and review the effectiveness of the system during the previous period.

Definitions and Acronyms QMS: Quality Management System
Procedure Introduction
Quality objectives are concrete, defined and measurable goals and targets that represent reasonable improvements, affecting the product or the quality system, in terms of:
Compliance with the Quality Policy, Effectiveness of the quality management system,
Degree of customer satisfaction, The execution, methodology or instrumentation of the different processes carried out for the provision of the service.
A possible combination of the above.
They usually focus on the improvement of:
The rates of complaints, non-conformities, corrective actions, etc.., Degree of customer satisfaction,
Indicators of quality, effectiveness or efficiency of the processes, activities or performances carried out during or after the provision of the service, which will result in greater satisfaction of both external and internal customers.
Quality objectives On an annual basis, management meets with the Quality Manager to set the annual QMS objectives, following a review of the effectiveness of the system during the previous period.
Reviewing the effectiveness of the system The review will consist of analysing:
The effectiveness of the Quality Management System, taking into account information on deviations, non-compliance with the Quality System, non-conformities that gave rise to corrective or preventive actions, etc.
The degree of customer satisfaction, based on the results obtained from the surveys carried out periodically through the questionnaires used to measure customer satisfaction and on the complaints received during the review period.
Opportunities for improvement, based on the information received through the different procedures that help us to evaluate the system: internal audits, complaints, deviations, inefficiency indicators, product quality reviews, possible notifications of non-conformities, etc...
New or revised regulatory requirements, where the Responsible Technician, Technician or person delegated by him shall verify the updates of the regulations applicable to the Quality System and shall be responsible for the implementation of the applicable changes.
Based on this review, the annual quality objectives and the individual objectives of the responsible personnel are set.
A report shall be drawn up by the Quality Manager on an annual basis and shall contain:
As input elements:
Customer feedback Handling of complaints
Notifications to the Health Regulatory Authority Audit results (if applicable)
Monitoring and measuring processes Product tracking and measurement
Corrective actions Preventive actions
Follow-up to previous reviews System changes
Recommendations for improvement New or revised regulatory requirements (including requirements derived from medical devices).
Information on notifications to health authorities (including requirements arising from medical devices).
As output elements:
Improvements necessary to maintain the suitability, adequacy and effectiveness of the QMS and its processes Product improvement in relation to customer requirements
Changes necessary to respond to new or revised applicable regulatory requirements Resource requirements
Outcome of the review The results of the review of the effectiveness of the QMS are used to set objectives for the next period including:
Improvements necessary to maintain the effectiveness of the QMS and its processes.
Product improvement in relation to customer requirements The resources needed to achieve them
