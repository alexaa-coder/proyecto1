JOB DESCRIPTIONS Aim
The objective of this procedure is to describe the main functions performed in the jobs of the sections or departments involved in Spika Tech, hereinafter SPIKA.
Scope This procedure applies to departments reporting to the CEO, according to the organization's approved organizational chart.
Responsibilities and Departments Affected It is the responsibility of management, or those they delegate to, to explain the content of their job to each person.
It is the responsibility of each employee's direct supervisor to prepare the Job Description.
It is the responsibility of the Technical Manager to ensure compliance with job descriptions and safeguard the documents generated.
The affected departments are those indicated in the following table:
Definitions and Acronyms DPT : Job Description
Procedure Each SPIKA employee will be provided with a DPT describing the duties and responsibilities they will have in their position. This DPT will be prepared by the individual's direct supervisor, or a delegated representative, and the employee will sign and review it.
Every employee must read the procedures applicable to their position, both those required by the quality management system and those specific to their position, all in accordance with PNT-003-Staff Training.
The direct manager or the person delegated will prepare the DPT according to ANNEX 1 Job Description and will take as reference the annexes of this procedure where the job description models are found, which will be completed or modified when necessary, individualizing it for each employee as appropriate.
Distribution of the Procedure Staff members in the departments listed in Section 3 (Responsibilities and Affected Departments) must be provided with a copy of this procedure. In addition, controlled copy No. 1 will be submitted to the Archives for paper archiving.
