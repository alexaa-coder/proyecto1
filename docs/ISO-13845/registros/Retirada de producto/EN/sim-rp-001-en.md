# Aim

Verify the effectiveness of the product recall procedure (PNT-008),
including the technical mechanisms for disabling and communicating with
internal users.

# Simulated scenario

Reason for withdrawal: A critical flaw has been detected that
compromises unauthorized access to user data.

Risk assessment: High.

Affected version: 1.0.0

Date of the drill: 10/07/2025

# Actions taken

-   Identification of the affected version, responsible for the action:
    IT

    -   The version with the bug has been located in the database.



-   Technical disqualification, responsible for the action: IT

    -   The access parameter has been modified to prevent its use.



-   Blocking verification, responsible for the action: IT

    -   An access attempt test has been performed



-   Internal Communication, responsible for the action: Quality

    -   The departments involved are informed



# 

# Results

Was access to the affected version successfully blocked? Yes

Were the affected departments notified? Yes

Conclusion on the effectiveness of the procedure: The simulation was
carried out satisfactorily, meeting the planned timeframe and
demonstrating that the PNT-008 procedure is effective for managing
software withdrawals or disabling. The affected version was correctly
identified and remotely disabled, preventing access by simulated users.
Internal communication worked adequately, and the departments involved
responded as expected.
