## **1. Aim**

The objective of this report is to establish SPIKA TECH's strategic and quality objectives for
2025, aligned with the quality policy, the strategic business plan, and applicable regulatory
requirements.

## **2. Scope**


The scope of this report applies to all departments of SPIKA TECH.

## **3. Regulatory and reference framework**


  - ISO 9001:2015 – Quality Systems.


  - ISO 13485:2018 – Medical Devices: Quality Management Systems. Requirements for
regulatory purposes.


  - ISO 14971:2019 – Medical Devices: Application of risk management to medical
devices.

## **5. Monitoring and evaluation**

Those responsible for each objective will review the progress of the objectives within the
stipulated timeframe. The status of compliance will be documented, and corrective measures
will be defined when significant deviations are detected.

## **6. Conclusions**


SPIKA TECH is committed to achieving the objectives set for 2025 and ensuring continuous
improvement of its management system and product performance.


At the end of 2025, a report will be prepared analyzing the achievement of the strategic and
quality objectives defined here.
