Description of the finding
The change handling SOP does not address impacts on MDR
compliance, risk management, UDI-DI, or reporting to the Notified
Body.


Root cause analysis
The change management procedure does not explicitly
address the assessment of the impact on compliance with the MDR, nor on
critical elements such as risk management, UDI-DI or the need for
notification to the Notified Body. This omission is due to the fact that
the procedure was initially adapted from a generic model without
integrating all the specific requirements applicable to medical devices
under the MDR, especially at an early stage of quality system
development.


Immediate correction
Record the non-conformity in the quality system and
provide traceability of the follow-up.


Corrective/preventive action
Review and update the change control procedure
to:
• Include a specific section on regulatory impact assessment
(MDR).
• Add fields in the change request template to record the risk
management impact analysis, UDI identifiers, technical documentation and
the need for notification to HTCert.
• Establish a checkpoint to review whether the change affects
information already assessed by the Notified Body.
