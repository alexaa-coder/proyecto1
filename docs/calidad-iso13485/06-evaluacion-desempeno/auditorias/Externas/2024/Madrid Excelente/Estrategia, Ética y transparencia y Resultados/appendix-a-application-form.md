**Specific project objectives** (max 2000 characters)







VR-CARDIO is a pioneering and disruptive, cost-effective solution
that integrates 3D-360º Stereoscopic and Holographic visualization with
non-invasive technology to capture cardiac signals. This creates an
electro anatomical map, specifically tailored for the monitoring and
diagnosis of arrhythmias, thus transforming the landscape of arrhythmia
care. This system reduces patient risks and significantly cuts costs in
areas such as catheterization and intervention expenses. 
VR-CARDIO Objectives: 

Implement VR-CARDIO in public healthcare systems and private
entities: Our aim is to improve patients' cardiovascular health through
early detection of cardiac arrhythmias. This objective will be measured
by quantitative indicators regarding the adoption of this
solution. 
Increase brand awareness and product adoption: Raise awareness of
the VR-CARDIO brand in the market and encourage product adoption among
professionals. This will be measured by metrics such as the number of
product description downloads, participation in marketing events, and
the conversion rate from trial users to regular users. 
Generate sustainable return on investment for investors: Our goal
is to provide an attractive and sustainable return on investment for
investors supporting the VR-CARDIO project. This will be achieved
through a solid business model and a detailed financial plan ensuring
long-term profitability. Specific goals for return on investment and
profitability will be established to meet investors'
expectations. 
Create strategic alliances and secure adequate funding: We will
identify and establish strategic alliances with financial providers and
investors who share our vision and are committed to sustainability. The
objective is to secure the necessary funding for the successful
implementation of the VR-CARDIO project, which will be measured by the
number of secured investments and the diversification of funding
sources. 

These specific objectives are clear, measurable, realistic, and
achievable during the project's duration. Key performance indicators
(KPIs) will be established to assess progress toward these objectives,
and strategic adjustments will be made as necessary to ensure the
success of the VR-CARDIO project and investor satisfaction.
To achieve these objectives, we are applying for this grant
opportunity to obtain specialized advisory services for:
a) Preparation of sustainable investment plans and related
documentation needed for receiving sustainable investments:  Business
Model.  Financial Plan for the business model case.  Business Valuation.
Sensitivity Analysis (Montecarlo or other).
b) Advisory and support in the Financial Plan implementation and
investment process: Investment proposal for financial providers and
investors. Identification of financial providers and investors. Support
in preparation of meetings with financial providers and
investors.

To estimate the potential reduction effect of CO2 after the
implementation of the VR-CARDIO investment project, a comprehensive
analysis of the direct and indirect environmental impacts of
project-related activities will be conducted. Some of the key
considerations for this estimation will include:

Energy Consumption: The energy consumption associated with the
operation of VR-CARDIO technology will be evaluated, including the use
of virtual reality devices, data servers, processing systems, and
information storage, among others. The carbon footprint associated with
the electricity generation necessary to power these operations will be
calculated. 
Physical Displacement: The effect of reducing CO2 emissions
resulting from the physical displacement of professional users will be
considered. Since VR-CARDIO offers a virtual reality solution, it is
expected that professional users may engage in diagnostic collaboration
activities in a virtual environment instead of traveling to physical
facilities, which may result in a decrease in greenhouse gas emissions
related to transportation.
Material Resources Reduction: The reduction of CO2 emissions
associated with the decrease in the use of material resources and waste
production will be evaluated. This may include reducing the consumption
of paper, plastic, and other materials used in traditional healthcare
documentation and management, as well as reducing medical waste
generated by invasive procedures. 
Life Cycle Analysis: A comprehensive life cycle analysis of
VR-CARDIO technology will be conducted, from device manufacturing to
disposal at the end of its useful life. This will allow the
identification of opportunities to optimize production processes, reduce
the use of materials and energy, and minimize environmental impact at
all stages of the product life cycle.

Once this information has been collected and analysed, a quantitative
estimation of the potential CO2 reduction effect because of the
implementation of the VR-CARDIO investment project will be made. This
estimation will be based on empirical data and validated scientific
models and will be used to communicate the environmental benefits of the
project to stakeholders and potential investors.

arrhythmias, ensuring more precise and efficient medical care.
Integration of Emerging Technologies: We will integrate emerging

quicker response to patients' needs.

models that ensure attractive long-term return on investment for
investors. This will involve identifying sustainable financing sources,
as well as implementing ethical and socially responsible business
practices.
Value Stream Management: We will implement value stream
management processes to optimize operational efficiency and reduce
costs. This will include identifying and eliminating waste in production
and delivery processes, leading to greater profitability and
competitiveness in the market. 
Management and Quality Standards: We will adopt internationally
recognized management and quality standards to ensure excellence in all
aspects of VR-CARDIO's operation. This will include implementing ISO
quality management systems and adopting best practices in project
management and customer service.
Energy Efficiency: We will implement energy efficiency solutions
in our operations to reduce our environmental impact and promote
sustainability. This will include adopting energy-efficient technologies
and optimizing our processes to minimize the consumption of natural
resources.

In summary, VR-CARDIO will be based on technological innovation,
economic and environmental sustainability, and best management practices
to achieve its objectives. By embracing cutting-edge technologies,
developing sustainable business models, and optimizing our operational
processes, we aim to transform the care of cardiac arrhythmias and
deliver positive outcomes for both patients and investors.
