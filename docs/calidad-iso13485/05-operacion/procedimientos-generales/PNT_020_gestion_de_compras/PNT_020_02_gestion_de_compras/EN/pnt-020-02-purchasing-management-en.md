# Target

The purpose of this procedure is to define the procedure for making
purchases at Spika Tech, hereinafter referred to as Spika.

# 

# Outreach

This procedure applies to all acquisitions made by SPIKA TECH that may
have an impact on:

-   The quality of the medical product or service

-   information security (confidentiality, integrity, availability)

-   The operation of the IT systems used in the development, manufacture
    or provision of the service.

#

# Definitions and Acronyms

-   IT (Information Technology): Information Technology

-   Critical supplier: Supplier whose product or service may
    affect regulatory compliance, patient safety or information
    security.

-   ISO 13485: International Standard for Quality Management
    Systems for Medical Devices

-   ISO/IEC 27001: International standard for information
    security management systems

-   NDA: Non-Disclosure Agreement

-   Security risk: The potential for an asset or service to
    adversely affect the confidentiality, integrity or availability of
    information.

# 

# Procedure

# **Identification of the need**

-   Technical description of the good or service

-   Justification

-   Expected impact (quality, security, IT)

-   Functional and technical requirements

# Impact and risk assessment

-   A risk analysis is carried out if the good/service is affected:

-   To the medical product

-   To information security

-   To critical IT systems

-   The assessment is documented by the Quality or Information Security
    Officer, as appropriate.

# **Supplier selection and evaluation**

The supplier must be previously evaluated according to the supplier
evaluation procedure, if it is considered critical for the product
developed by SPIKA.

In case of a critical supplier, they should be considered:

-   ISO Certifications

-   Confidentiality agreements (NDA)

-   Security policies

-   Compliance history

# Request for quotations

The Purchasing Manager requests a minimum of two quotations. Exceptions
justified by availability or specialisation are allowed. IT Department
reviews technical requirements and compatibility. Quality and
Information Security review if applicable.

# Approval of the acquisition

Approval is granted by the Purchasing Manager and/or Management, after
validation:

-   Technical requirements

-   Risk assessment

-   Regulatory compliance

# Reception and verification

Once the product or service has been received:

-   IT performs tests and verifies specifications

-   Quality verifies compliance if applicable to the quality system.

-   Information Security validates agreed measures (passwords, access,
    etc.).
