# Procedure

# Introduction 

On an annual basis, questionnaires will be sent out by the Sales
Department in order to measure the satisfaction of the customers to whom
SPIKA supplies products.

A selection of the customers with the highest turnover will be made and
the questionnaire included in **Annex 1** will be sent to them and
returned to the Responsible Technician. The selection of customers and
follow-up of the questionnaires will be carried out by the Sales area.

In this annual evaluation SPIKA will assess the post-production
activities by reviewing the following records:

-   List of customer complaints, to be documented in the IRSGCD

-   Customer satisfaction questionnaires

# Sending questionnaires to customers.

With the list of customers with the highest turnover, the Sales staff
will send the "**Customer Satisfaction Questionnaire**" (see **Annex**
1**) by post or e-mail to the customers indicated in the list, in order**
to be able to evaluate the quality of the product and service.

The **"Customer Satisfaction Questionnaire**" includes 3 sections for
the assessment of:

-   **Product** Satisfaction.

-   **Service** Satisfaction.

-   Overall satisfaction.

# Assessment of the questionnaire

Once the duly completed questionnaire has been received, it will be sent
to the Responsible Technician. The questionnaires must be signed and
dated, or, failing this, the communication of the sending of the
questionnaire to SPIKA must be included.

With these questionnaires and the annual complaint lists, all
information will be reviewed and conclusions will be collected in the
IRSGC.

# Communication to management

The result of the customer satisfaction measurement shall be reported to
the management in accordance with **SOP-010 Management review of the**
effectiveness of the quality management system**.**
