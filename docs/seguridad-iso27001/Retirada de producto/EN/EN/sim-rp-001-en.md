# Results

Was access to the affected version successfully blocked? Yes

Were the affected departments notified? Yes

Conclusion on the effectiveness of the procedure: The simulation was
carried out satisfactorily, meeting the planned timeframe and
demonstrating that the PNT-008 procedure is effective for managing
software withdrawals or disabling. The affected version was correctly
identified and remotely disabled, preventing access by simulated users.
Internal communication worked adequately, and the departments involved
responded as expected.
