Application review

The conformity assessment under MDR is a
multi-step process, starting with the review of
the application and, upon its positive outcome,
proceeding to the assessment of the devices’
Technical Documentation and the
manufacturer’s Quality Management System.

The positive outcome of this review is a
prerequisite for the acceptance of the
application by HTCert and the signing of the
contract. A negative outcome will lead to the
rejection of the application. In this scenario,
HTCert will enter into the EUDAMED database
the relevant data and the process will be
terminated. However, a new application may be
lodged, starting a new cycle of review and
assessment.


Assessment process

After acceptance of the application, the binding
contract will be signed and the conformity
assessment process will start.

The conformity assessment includes
assessment of the technical documentation in
conjunction with audit of the quality
management system. A detailed description of
all the process steps and their sequence,
covering both the initial assessment and the
monitoring activities during the validity period of
the certification, is provided in the General
Terms and Conditions for the Certification of
Medical Devices under MDR, which you can
[download following the link General-Terms.](https://htcert.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/General-Terms-and-Conditions-for-MDR-Certification-1.pdf)

Please keep in mind that Greek and English are
the only acceptable languages for
communication with HTCert and submitted data
must be into one of these languages.
