## **1. Aim**


The objective of this report is to conduct a review of the Quality Management System by the
Management of SPIKA TECH, hereinafter SPIKA, in 2024.

## **2. Scope**


The scope of this document is SPIKA's quality system, in compliance with its commitment to
quality and the achievement of regulatory requirements and PNT010 – Review of the Quality
Management System by Management.

## **3. Definitions and Acronyms**


QMS: Quality Management System.


AI: from English, artificial intelligence.


ND: Not available


NA: Not Applicable

## **4. Development** **4.1. Introduction**


SPIKA is a newly established organization in terms of regulatory compliance for medical
devices. It has a quality system implemented by 2024. This report aims to indicate, based on
the quality records obtained during the year of system implementation, the organization's
level of compliance with it.


This report will analyze:


   - The effectiveness of the Quality Management System

   - Degree of Customer Satisfaction

   - Opportunities for Improvement

   - New or revised regulatory requirements

## **4.3. Output elements**


**4.3.1.** **Improvements needed to maintain the suitability, adequacy and effectiveness**

**of the QMS and its processes**


Based on the results, no improvements are proposed to enhance the quality management
system, as it requires more time to implement and requires feedback from users. The next
version of the report will indicate any improvements to the QMS, if applicable.


**4.3.2.** **Product improvement in relation to customer requirements**


At the time of writing this report, the product was not yet in distribution, so customer feedback
was not available. The next version of the report will indicate, where applicable, any proposed
improvements and their traceability to internal actions.


**4.3.3.** **Changes required to respond to new or revised applicable regulatory**

**requirements**


No regulatory changes have been identified that affect the SGC.


**4.3.4.** **Resource needs**


It is not considered necessary to implement improvements to current human or infrastructure
resources.

## **5. Review result**


Based on the review, SPIKA is a company with a software product that has recently
implemented a Quality Management System. It is completing the validation and testing
process for its product in order to continue the process of registering it and making it available
to its end customers.
