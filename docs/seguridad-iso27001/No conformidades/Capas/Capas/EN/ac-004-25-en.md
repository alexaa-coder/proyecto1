&lt;td>Description of the finding&lt;/td>
&lt;td colspan="12">The issues related with the UDI (basic udi-di, udi-pi
etc) are not clearly addressed in the QMS. (E.g. It is not described the
impact of versioning to the udi-pi, or the impact of change of the
intended purpose of the product to the basic udi-di)&lt;/td>
&lt;/tr>
&lt;tr class="odd">
&lt;td>Root cause analysis&lt;/td>
&lt;td colspan="12">The lack of clear development on UDI-related aspects
(including Basic UDI-DI, UDI-DI and UDI-PI) within the quality
management system is due to a limited initial interpretation of the MDR
requirements in this area. In particular, the impacts of certain changes
on the unique identifier, such as changes in the version/revision of the
software (UDI-PI) or changes in the intended use of the product (Basic
UDI-DI), are not adequately addressed.&lt;/td>
&lt;/tr>
&lt;tr class="even">
&lt;td>Immediate correction&lt;/td>
&lt;td colspan="12">Record the non-conformity in the quality system and
provide traceability of the follow-up.&lt;/td>
&lt;/tr>
&lt;tr class="odd">
&lt;td>Corrective/preventive action&lt;/td>
&lt;td colspan="12">Modification of the labelling control procedure to
include:
&lt;ul>
&lt;li>Clear definition of UDI-DI, UDI-PI and Basic UDI-DI
elements&lt;/li>
&lt;li>Records and traceability of UDI code assignment.&lt;/li>
&lt;li>Specification of the person responsible for generating,
maintaining and communicating UDI codes.&lt;/li>
&lt;/ul>
Update of the change control procedure to:
&lt;ul>
&lt;li>Include UDI impact analysis for every modification.&lt;/li>
&lt;li>Document when a release generates a new UDI-PI.&lt;/li>
&lt;li>Include criteria for determining when a change affects the Basic
UDI-DI (e.g. change in intended use or product family).&lt;/li>
&lt;/ul>&lt;/td>
&lt;/tr>
