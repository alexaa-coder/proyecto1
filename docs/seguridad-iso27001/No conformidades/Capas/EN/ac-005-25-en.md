&lt;td>Description of the finding&lt;/td>
&lt;td colspan="12">The SOP PNT-030 for Strategy for regulatory compliance
does not clearly describes the identification of relevant legal
requirements, the identification of the sources as well as the
responsibilities for the tasks. In addition the follow up of CS and/or
harmonized standards is not covered&lt;/td>
&lt;/tr>
&lt;tr class="odd">
&lt;td>Root cause analysis&lt;/td>
&lt;td colspan="12">Procedure PNT-030 does not explicitly and in a
structured way address the mechanisms to:
&lt;ul>
&lt;li>Identify applicable legal requirements (MDR, national
legislation, etc.).&lt;/li>
&lt;li>Determine the official sources of information (EU Official
Journal, European Commission website, notified bodies, industry
associations).&lt;/li>
&lt;li>Assign clear responsibilities for monitoring these
requirements.&lt;/li>
&lt;li>Monitor changes in Common Specifications (CS) or relevant
harmonised standards.&lt;/li>
&lt;li>This omission is due to the fact that the procedure was initially
drafted early in the development of the system, and was not revised
after the full entry into force of the MDR.&lt;/li>
&lt;/ul>&lt;/td>
&lt;/tr>
&lt;tr class="even">
&lt;td>Immediate correction&lt;/td>
&lt;td colspan="12">Record the non-conformity in the quality system and
provide traceability of the follow-up.&lt;/td>
&lt;/tr>
&lt;tr class="odd">
&lt;td>Corrective/preventive action&lt;/td>
&lt;td colspan="12">Revision of procedure PNT-030, including:
&lt;ul>
&lt;li>Specific section for the identification of applicable legal and
regulatory requirements, including MDR, applicable directives, national
legislation, and MDCG guidelines.&lt;/li>
&lt;li>Inclusion of a list of official sources of updated information
(websites, bulletins, standards databases, etc.).&lt;/li>
&lt;li>Clear assignment of responsible parties for monitoring, reviewing
and updating these requirements.&lt;/li>
&lt;li>Description of the monitoring process for harmonised standards
and Common Specifications (CS), including frequency, monitoring tools
(e.g. CEN notifications, OJEU) and criteria for assessing their
applicability.&lt;/li>
&lt;li>Integration with the risk assessment and change management
procedure to ensure that the system is updated when requirements
change.&lt;/li>
&lt;/ul>&lt;/td>
